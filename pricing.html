Best Seller

The zipcode with the highest amount of activity is 28212.

Buying Season

The majority of home sales occur during the summer.

Average Price

From 2017 to 2021, the average listing price varies through the years mostly in the positive, though some areas experienced less growth than the others. 28207 sits at the highest pricing, with 28213 sitting at the bottom with very little change. To see the changes, select the zipcodes you would like to see in the chart using the buttons on the right. You can hover your mouse over the lines at any point to see the data for that zipcode.

Market Hotness: Supply vs. Demand

A composite score of Supply and Demand scores, with Supply being based on median days a listing is on the market and Demand on total listing views, it indicates the flow of real estate sales within the given area.

What is the trend?

If pricing continues on its current path, the cost of housing will increase dramatically from decade to decade. By 2070, the median cost of a home would be $1.4 million.

Price shifts

Selling and listing prices were constantly shifting from 2017 to 2021, with the median selling price never reaching the initial listed value.

Home is where the heart is, but once we move away the home remains to be listed. From July 2016 to April 2021, the amount of listed homes were in constant flux.
